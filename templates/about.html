About CYBERHUB

Our Digital Vision

CYBERHUB is a curated digital arcade, a beacon for those who revel in the glow of neon and the hum of archaic processors. We aim to provide a seamless, immersive gateway to a collection of games that capture the essence of the cyberpunk genre. This is more than just a website; it's an experience, a dive into dystopian futures where technology and rebellion collide.

The Cyberpunk Aesthetic

Our design philosophy is deeply rooted in the cyberpunk ethos: high-tech, low-life. Expect a visual tapestry woven from dark, moody palettes, vibrant neon accents, and typography that echoes the digital frontier. Every element is crafted to transport you to sprawling megacities and underground havens.

Technology Stack

This hub is built with a blend of modern web technologies:

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Python (Flask)
  • Database: SQLite (for future scorekeeping)

We prioritize a lightweight, fast, and accessible experience across devices.
